Welcome to Smart Study Planner

Smart Study Planner is a personalized AI-powered study management tool designed to help students organize their tasks efficiently.

Key Features

  • Smart Scheduling: Generates personalized study plans based on deadlines and subject difficulty.
  • Task Management: Add, edit, and track study tasks effortlessly.
  • Interactive Calendar: View upcoming tasks and deadlines in a structured manner.
  • Reminders and Notifications: Stay on top of your study schedule with reminders.

How to Use

  • Navigate: Use the sidebar to switch between Home, Profile, Dashboard, Settings, and Help.
  • Add Work: Click on the “+ Add Work” button in the calendar to add a new study task.
  • View Schedule: Check the calendar for upcoming deadlines and study sessions.
  • Customize: Modify your study plan based on subject priority and deadlines.
